About MacKenzie Mortgages

Our services include:
  • Advising and arranging your mortgage
  • Guiding you through the House buying Process
  • Liaising with your solicitor
  • Advising and arranging mortgage related insurances e.g Buildings and Contents Insurance, Life and Critical Illness Cover, Accident, Sickness and Unemployment Protection, Income Protection.
  • For Buildings & Contents Insurance and Accident, Sickness & Unemployment Insurance we offer products from a selected panel of providers.
We specialise in offering advice on mortgages for the following:
  • 1st Time Buyers
  • ‘LIFT’ – Government’s Shared Equity Scheme
  • Shared Ownership Mortgages
  • New Build Houses and Flats
  • Buy to Let Investors
  • Let to Buy Mortgages
  • Remortgages
  • Home Movers
  • Self Builds
  • Energy Efficient Housing
  • Ecological Renovations
  • Holiday Let Mortgages
Other services which can be arranged are (by referral):
  • Commerical Mortgages
  • Business Protection
  • Investments
  • Pensions
  • ISAs
  • Help with Inheritance Tax Planning
  • Private Medical Insurance
  • Secured Loans (Second Charge Mortgages)

    • These services are available through the introduction to other specialist advisers.

We also can put you in touch with a specialist insurance independent broker who can offer insurance quotes to you instantly from car insurance to pet insurance.

The Financial Conduct Authority does not regulate Will writing, Commercial mortgages, solicitors, surveyors and some forms of Buy to Let mortgages and Inheritance Tax Planning.
